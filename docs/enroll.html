Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission?

The following documents are required: Birth certificate, previous academic records, transfer certificate (if applicable), passport-sized photographs, and address proof.

What is the admission timeline?

Our admission process typically begins in July and concludes by September. Early applications are encouraged as seats are limited.

Is there an entrance examination?

Yes, depending on the grade level. The entrance assessment evaluates the student's aptitude and knowledge appropriate for their age group.
