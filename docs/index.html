Nurturing Future Leaders: Discover Our Values and Vision

B.D. International Public School, established in 2010, is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering holistic development. Our mission is to nurture academic excellence and a lifelong love of learning in a stimulating environment. We strive to empower students with critical thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing them for a rapidly changing world. With core values of integrity, excellence, innovation, and community, we aim to create well-rounded individuals prepared for future success and global citizenship. We foster a culture of respect, responsibility, and resilience, encouraging students to reach their full potential. We envision being a leading school recognized for its innovative approach to education, setting a benchmark for academic excellence and character development.

Admissions

Admission Process

  1. Inquiry: Contact the admissions office for initial information.
  2. Application: Fill out the online application form.
  3. Document Submission: Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, etc.).
  4. Interview: Attend an interview with the admissions committee.
  5. Entrance Exam: Take the entrance exam (if applicable).
  6. Result Announcement: Check the admission list for the results.
  7. Fee Payment: Pay the admission fee to confirm enrollment.
